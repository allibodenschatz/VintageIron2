About Us



Vintage Iron Gym

For a lot of people, including myself, the gym is a place to escape from our current struggles, and the one dream I’ve always had was to open a gym with a great atmosphere and great equipment. So, I decided to put everything I have into chasing the dream of opening Vintage Iron.

Vintage Iron is a place to better yourself physically and mentally, and have the opportunity to train on hand-selected equipment from all over the states.

Here, you’ll find a variety of machines from quality brands from the industry, and even some rare ones that aren’t manufactured anymore.

The mission for Vintage Iron is to continue to improve the facility by bringing in new machines and other equipment for members.

Sometimes, all you need is the person looking back at you in the mirror to help you get where you want to be. But when you surround yourself with like-minded people who want the absolute best for you, great things will happen.
